One Shade is a color puzzle game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. Your game progress (scores, levels, coins) is processed entirely on your device and is never shared with us.

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain device information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device type and operating system version

For more details, please review Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Storage

All game data and progress are stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data. Uninstalling the app will remove all local data.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
